关于serialize()方法会把空格替换为加号的解决办法

jQuery的serialize()方法会把空格替换为加号；

示例

例如原来是 "a b"，调用serialize()之后就变成了"a++b"

解决办法

var editHolidayForm = $("#editHolidayForm").serialize();
editHolidayForm = editHolidayForm.replace(/\+/g," ");//将加号再替换成空格...
editHolidayForm = decodeURIComponent(editHolidayForm,true);
